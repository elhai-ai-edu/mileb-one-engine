## A Few Words About the Project

I'm developing a system called Ed421 — a platform designed for personalized skill assessment, recommendation, and learning, focusing on academic and career-related competencies for students, graduates, and professionals in the workforce.

The goal is to create a personalized process that includes:
1. Skill Assessment Process:
- Diagnostic questionnaire
- Answer processing and generation of a personal report
- Recommendation of a learning path

2. Personal Learning Journey:
- Supportive guidance and AI-driven learning assistant throughout the process

All of this is based on the GPT API, integrated with tools such as Tally, Google Sheets, Notion, and Make.

## Meeting Goals

I am looking for professional guidance at the MVP stage of the system, with the aim to:
- Understand how to build an AI-based system that is stable, scalable, and future-proof.
- Distinguish between quick, efficient solutions and “dead-end” approaches.
- Build a process that enables future expansion — both in terms of user volume and AI sophistication.

## Topics I’d Like to Discuss During Our Meeting

### 1. Integration of Different AI Components

- When is a GPT chatbot sufficient?
- When should I integrate other models like Generative AI, NLP, or sentiment/language analysis?
- Should GPT be seen as a first step, followed by more advanced models?

### 2. Data Strategy

- How can I structure the data to avoid future roadblocks?
- Is Google Sheets enough for now? When would I need a data warehouse?
- How do I maintain consistency and reliability in tags/IDs?

### 3. Monitoring & Output Quality

- How can I assess the quality of GPT’s responses?
- Should I collect user feedback or ratings for each report?
- Is it possible to implement A/B testing or other metrics?

### 4. Privacy, Ethics, and Regulations

- Does GPT store user data, and is that a regulatory concern?
- How can I ensure the data remains anonymous?

### 5. Adapting to Diverse Populations

- How can I ensure the language is user-friendly for Arabic, Russian, and English speakers?
- Is there mobile support? Accessibility for visually impaired users or users with learning disabilities?

### 6. Smart, Gradual Development

- What technical decisions at the MVP stage will support smooth growth later on?
- Which choices are likely to “lock me in,” and which remain flexible?

### 7. Future Integration with Moodle (Cautiously)

- Should I aim for Moodle integration?
- Or leave that completely for a future phase?

### 8. The Role of the Bot Along the Journey

- Should I think of the bot as a personal assistant guiding the user from assessment through learning support?
- How should that be implemented technically? (via the API?) Do I need chat logs? Memory?

### 9. AI Use in the Research Layer

- How can GPT assist in analyzing users’ open-ended responses qualitatively?
- Can I generate automatic periodic reports?
- Can the assessment phase feed directly into the research process (e.g., reliability, user attitudes, effectiveness)?
